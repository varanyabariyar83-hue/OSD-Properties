Who We Are

Built on Trust,
Driven by Excellence

OSD Properties & Developers is a premier real estate company based in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, with over 15 years of experience helping families and businesses find the right property in the right location.

Founded by Namana Tripathi, OSD Properties & Developers has established itself as a trusted name in both residential and commercial real estate across the Yamuna Expressway corridor, Alpha, Beta, and Gamma sectors of Greater Noida.

We believe every property transaction is a life milestone. Our team of experts ensures end-to-end support — from site visits and legal due diligence to registration and possession.
