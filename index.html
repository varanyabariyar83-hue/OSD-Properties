Who We Are

Built on Trust

OSD Properties & Developers is a premier real estate company based in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, with over 15 years of experience helping families and businesses find the right residential, commercial, and industrial property across Greater Noida and the Yamuna Expressway Corridor.

Founded by Ashutosh Kumar Singh and Namana Tripathi, OSD Properties & Developers has established itself as a trusted name in residential, commercial, and industrial real estate. Legal Compliance

All Plots Approved by Authorised Bodies

Every residential, commercial, and industrial plot we deal in is fully approved and regulated by the official development authorities governing Greater Noida and the Yamuna Expressway Corridor - ensuring complete legal compliance and peace of mind.

Active Approval

Greater Noida Authority

Greater Noida Industrial Development Authority (GNIDA)

Residential plots across Alpha, Beta, Gamma, Delta, Eta, Zeta, Omicron, Mu, Xu, Sector-36, Sector-37, Sigma, Swarnagri, P3, P4, Chi Phi sectors are GNIDA approved with clear titles and verified ownership.

Primary Authority
Active Approval

YEIDA

Yamuna Expressway Industrial Development Authority

Governing body for all plots along the Yamuna Expressway Corridor - Sector 18, Sector 20, Sector 22D, Sector 25 (7% Kisan Quota) - including investment zones near Jewar International Airport and Noida Film City.

Active Approval

UPSIDA

Uttar Pradesh State Industrial Development Authority

All industrial plots in Greater Noida are developed and approved under UPSIDA's industrial development framework - fully compliant and investment-ready.

✅

OSD Properties & Developers only deals in properties with clear, verified titles approved by these authorities. All documentation is transparent and legally sound before any transaction.
